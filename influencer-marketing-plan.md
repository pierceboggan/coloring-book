## üì± Platform-Specific Strategies

### **Instagram Strategy**

#### **Content Formats:**
1. **Before/After Carousel Posts**
   - Slide 1: Original family photo
   - Slide 2: Generated coloring page
   - Slide 3: Colored-in result by kids
   - Slide 4: App screenshot/logo

2. **Stories Content**
   - Time-lapse of upload process
   - Kids coloring the generated page
   - "Swipe up" links (for accounts with link feature)

3. **Reels Content**
   - Quick transformation videos (photo ‚Üí coloring page)
   - Kids' reactions to seeing their coloring page
   - Side-by-side coloring sessions

#### **Hashtag Strategy:**
- **Primary**: #coloringbook #familytime #kidsactivities #momlife #dadlife
- **Secondary**: #homeschool #teacherlife #screenfreetime #familyfun
- **Niche**: #coloringtherapy #kidscrafts #artforkids #creativekids

### **TikTok Strategy**

#### **Content Formats:**
1. **Transformation Videos (15-30 seconds)**
   - Upload photo ‚Üí AI processing ‚Üí reveal coloring page
   - Trending audio: "Wait for it..." or transformation sounds

2. **Reaction Videos**
   - Kids seeing their photo become a coloring page
   - Parents surprised by the quality
   - Before/after reveals

3. **Tutorial/Demo Content**
   - "How to turn any photo into a coloring book"
   - "Screen-free activity hack for parents"
   - "Teachers, save this for rainy day activities"

#### **TikTok Trending Tactics:**
- Use trending sounds and remix them with transformation content
- Jump on parenting/teacher trend hashtags
- Create "POV" content ("POV: You need to entertain your kids for 2 hours")

## üîç Influencer Outreach Strategy

### **Finding Influencers:**

#### **Research Tools:**
- **Instagram**: Search hashtags #momlife #teacherlife #kidsactivities
- **TikTok**: Browse "For You" page for parenting content
- **AspireIQ, Upfluence, or Creator.co** for database search
- **Manual research**: Look at competitors' tagged posts

#### **Outreach Template:**

```
Subject: Partnership Opportunity - Turn Photos into Coloring Pages! 

Hi [Name]!

I love your content about [specific recent post]. Your [kids/students/audience] would absolutely love what we've created!

We've built an AI app that transforms any photo into a beautiful coloring page in seconds. Imagine turning family photos, pet pictures, or classroom memories into custom coloring activities!

Would you be interested in trying it out? I'd love to send you:
- Free Pro access for 3 months
- Custom coloring pages made from your photos
- A collaboration opportunity if it's a good fit

No strings attached - just excited to share this with creators who'd genuinely love it!

Check out some examples: [link]

Best,
[Name]
```

### **Vetting Process:**
1. **Audience Alignment**: Check if followers match our target demographic
2. **Engagement Quality**: Look for genuine comments, not just likes
3. **Content Quality**: Professional-looking posts that align with brand
4. **Brand Safety**: Review recent posts for any controversial content
5. **Posting Frequency**: Active accounts that post regularly
