### Color Scheme

The AR Gallery uses the app's signature pastel color palette:

- **Primary Controls**: Coral Pink `#FF6B9D` (reset button)
- **Secondary Controls**: Aqua Blue `#A0E7E5` (camera button)
- **Accent**: Blush Pink `#FEC8D8` (size controls)
- **Text**: Dark Purple `#3A2E39`
- **Backgrounds**: White with 80-95% opacity
- **Borders**: Matching gradients with control colors
