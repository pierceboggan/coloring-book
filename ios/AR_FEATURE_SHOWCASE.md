## Key Interactions

### 1. Surface Detection
The app scans the environment and detects walls, floors, and tables where artwork can be placed.

### 2. Tap to Place
A single tap places the artwork on any detected surface, perfectly oriented to match the wall or floor.

### 3. Natural Gestures
- **Pinch**: Makes artwork bigger or smaller
- **Drag**: Moves artwork to a new location
- **Buttons**: Fine-tune size with +/- controls

### 4. Capture & Share
Take screenshots of your AR gallery to share with friends and family!
